Venture Definition

Initialize the assessment by defining the target venture parameters.

1. Awareness

"To what extent do potential customers recognize they have this specific problem?"

2. Clarity

"How clearly can this problem be described to target customers?"

3. Effort

"How much effort do customers currently expend to solve this problem?"

4. Frustration

"How frustrating is the current process of finding or using a solution?"

5. Value Perception

"How much value do customers believe they receive from a potential solution?"

6. Price Sensitivity

"How sensitive are customers to the price of a potential solution?"

7. TAM (Market Size)

"What is the total size of the addressable problem market?"

8. SAM (Serviceable Market)

"How much of this market can your channels realistically reach?"

9. SOM (Obtainable Market)

"How much of reachable market can you realistically capture?"
